
*****Instruction for Playing the Game*****

Rule 1: By default the level of the Game will be Level 1
Rule 2: You can change the Level of the Game under Level Option
Rule 3: Easy Level: You have to Guess a Number between 1 and 50
Rule 4: Medium Level: You have to Guess a Number between 1 and 100
Rule 5: Hard Level: You have to Guess a Number between 1 and 500
